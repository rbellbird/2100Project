About the 2100 Project Advisory Group

The Advisory Group helps oversee The 2100 Project by

  • contributing ideas for 2100 Project initiatives;
  • making suggestions for content, such as links to websites, quotes, ideas for images and related descriptions for the scrolling image gallery, and ideas for authors for monthly features;
  • sharing input about how to improve the website;
  • endorsing the general concepts behind the 2100 Project;
  • participating in meetings or Skype sessions to brainstorm about the project.
